Resources

Here is a short list of resources about science, ecology, and programming that I find particularly useful and interesting.

Science communication and knowledge transfer

Blogs

  • Le nutritionniste urbain: nutrition and environment (French)

  • Le Pharmachien: health (French)

Books

  • Data feminism by Catherine D’Ignazio and Lauren F. Klein: doing data science to dismantle systems of power

  • The climate book by Greta Thunberg et al: understanding and mitigating climate change

Podcasts

  • Les Lucioles: graduate students of the Biology Department (my colleagues!) discussing their research (French)

  • Ologies: various discussions about biology and culture

Youtube channels

  • 3Blue1Brown: mathematical concepts explained with animations

  • acapellascience: a cappella songs inspired by science and pop culture

  • L’Histoire nous le dira: general history knowledge (French)

  • Scilabus: science communication (French)

  • StatQuest: statistics, machine learning and data science thought with humor

Programming and data science

Textbooks and guides

  • Guides to better science: guides of the British Ecological Society to promote research excellence

  • Quarto: building a personal website using Quarto (was very helpful when I built mine)

  • Statistical rethinking by Richard McElreath: a (funny) Bayesian course with examples in R and Stan

  • Think Julia: programming in Julia like a data scientist

Data visualization and images

  • colorbrewer and coolers: generating beautiful color palettes with an option for colorblind safe

  • The R Graph Gallery: collection of charts with reproducible code

  • Top 50 ggplot2 visualizations: collection of charts made with ggplot2

  • PhyloPic: free silhouette images of animals and plants

  • Unsplash: outstanding pictures with a permissive license

Workshops

  • QCBS R workshops: introduction to R to multivariate analyses

  • The Carpentries: open-source programming workshops

Ecological and environmental data

Occurrence and distribution data

  • Biodiversité Québec: Quebec biodiversity monitoring

  • GBIF: global species occurrences

  • iNaturalist: species observations and identifications

  • IUCN: species range maps

  • OBIS: marine species occurrences

Species trait data

  • FishBase: fish traits

  • Open Traits Network: registry of species traits

  • Open Tree of Life: phylogenetic relationships

  • Plant Trait Database: plant traits

Species interaction data

  • Global Biotic Interactions (GloBI): species interactions

  • Mangal: ecological networks

Climate and geographic data

  • CHELSA: global climate of the earth’s land surface

  • Worldclim: global climate

Other databases

  • Data One: data repository

  • UCI Machine Learning Repository: toy datasets for machine learning

Miscellaneous

Humanities and geography

  • Native Land Digital: maps of Indigenous territories, treaties, and languages

  • TERMIUM Plus: robust English to French translations of terminology

  • what3words: 3 words that give your precise location

Recipes

  • La cuisine de Jean-Philippe: vegan recipes (French)

  • Loounie: vegan recipes (French)
